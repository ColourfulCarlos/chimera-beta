Supporting Chimera

Chimera is a free website and resource to use, without any paywalls, premium features or hidden capacity. Everything that Chimera provides is for everyone.

While this is my first project from scratch as a full-stack developer I want to continuously keep working on Chimera with the addition of new features, new ways to customize and new exciting ways to interconnect your Heroes on Chimera.

The most direct way to support development of this project is with Patreon, where you as a contributor will ensure the community gets regular Chimera updates with new features and bug fixes. You'll also get access to my Patreon activity stream, where you can follow directly the development of Chimera updates.

Why Ads?

Ads are an indirect but important way to support Chimera. While many players might use Chimera in their daily playtime not many will be able to contribute via Patreon for the continuity of this project. So to assure that at the very least the website is running and updated I made the decision of running 3 small ads in between sections to ensure that objective.

Please consider whitelisting this website from your adblocker, the ads are non-intrusive and such a small gesture will help me pay the bills related to Chimera.

The Chimera

Chimera is a community support website produced in a non-commercial environment, as such the current and future versions of Chimera will always be free and shareable throughout Marvel’s Avengers online community with the intent to be a common place to view, share and seek more information about the game.

Marvel's Avengers is built upon feedback from the community and likewise this project is open-sourced for anyone that wants to contribute and help the community. You can provide feedback directly on GitHub, or, you can always find me on Discord.

It's my intent for this to be one of the best third-party resources that the community can find, and I hope to achieve this with help of the community itself, so don’t forget to: use, share and support Chimera.

Team Behind Chimera

Hello Avengers, my name is Carlos, a recent Web Developer with a huge love for comic-books. I started this project in the hopes of practicing my new found powers but while playing during the Beta Weekend I knew instantly that I would spend hundreds and hundreds of hours in this marvelous universe.

I’m an avid player of Destiny, Division, World of Warcraft and many others, and I always loved the websites and apps that the community would build for others, so why not build my own from the experience and inspired by those community resources?

As I said earlier, I am quite recent in the whole Web Development world and as such, changes and problem fixing will take some time, bare with me while I develop my skills in this project that I know will give everything that I’m capable of.
